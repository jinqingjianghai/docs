## Product Overview

### 1. Introduction
Powered by leading foundation models, our platform offers **cost-effective GenAI services**.

### 2. Key Features
As an all-in-one cloud service platform integrating top-tier AI models, we aim to provide developers with **faster, cheaper, more comprehensive, and smoother** model APIs.

- A wide selection of models already available: Large Language Models, Text-to-Image Models, Code Generation Models, Vector & Re-ranking Models, and Multimodal Models.
- Developers and product teams can focus on innovation without worrying about compute costs during development or scaling, achieving true **"Token Freedom"**.
- Ready-to-use model inference acceleration services offer **faster and more efficient user experiences** for your GenAI applications.
